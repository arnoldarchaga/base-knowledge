# Lighting and Time of Day

Lighting can completely change the mood of a truck photo. Even if the truck is clean and the location is good, harsh lighting can make the picture look flat or messy. The best time to take photos is usually golden hour, which happens shortly after sunrise or before sunset. During this time, the light is softer and warmer, which makes the truck look better without needing heavy editing.

## Golden Hour

Golden hour works well because the sunlight is not directly above the truck. This helps avoid harsh shadows and bright reflections. For my F-150, golden hour would make the body lines, wheels, and front end stand out more. It also gives the photo a more natural and cinematic look.

### Night Photos

Night pictures can also look good, especially at gas stations, parking garages, or downtown areas. The key is making sure there is enough light on the truck so it does not look blurry or too dark.
