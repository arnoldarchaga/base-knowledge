# About This Knowledge Base

My name is Arnold Archaga, and I created this knowledge base to organize information about truck photography. I currently own an F-150 and want to improve the way I photograph it using my iPhone.

The purpose of this website is to provide practical information about camera basics, composition, lighting, planning, editing, and sharing photographs. It is designed for beginners who may not have professional camera equipment or another person available to take their pictures.

I chose truck photography because it is something I am personally interested in and want to continue improving. Creating this knowledge base also gave me a way to organize what I have learned into clear categories that are easy for other people to explore.

This project demonstrates how information can be organized using folders, descriptive page titles, internal links, consistent formatting, and clear navigation. Each section connects to related pages so visitors can move through the website without becoming lost.
